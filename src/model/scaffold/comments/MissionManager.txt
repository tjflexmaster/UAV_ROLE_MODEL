Function:			--all functions this class currently handles
	When the VGUI sends an alert goes to check the anomalies
	When Observing the VGUI to verify anomaly gives 75% likelihood to dismiss false
		 and pursue true anomalies and 25% likelihood to dismiss true and pursue false anomalies.
	Accepts target sighting info from VO and passes it to PS
	Accepts NewSearchAOI and TerminateSearch instructions from the PS and passes that to the OP
	Accepts TerminateSearch and TargetDescription info from PS and passes it to the VO
	Can handle the PS ending transmission without sending data
	When transmitting to PS if nothing to transmit just sends an end comm
	Accepts SearchComplete and SearchFailed info from OP and passes it to the PS
